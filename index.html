About

From building PCs at 13 to filing patents at 40.

I grew up taking computers apart in Houston. Studied finance and then an M.B.A. at the University of Houston (BBA Finance 2010, MBA 2015). Spent the next decade running marketing and IT for small businesses across the city — clinics, restaurants, retail, home services — the kind of work where one person has to be the strategist, the engineer, and the one who actually pushes the deploy.

That work eventually became the question behind Frequentor: why is it that the businesses we walk into every week know less about us than a website we’ve never visited? I’m building the answer with a small team out of Houston, backed by patents we’ve filed at the USPTO and credits from ElevenLabs, PostHog, NVIDIA, Lambda, AWS, and Anthropic.
