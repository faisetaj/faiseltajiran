02 — ABOUT

Builder by default. Marketer by training. Hospitality operator by domain. I learned to ship by being the only person in the room who could.

I grew up taking computers apart in Houston. BBA, then MBA from the University of Houston. I spent a decade running growth and IT for small businesses — clinics, restaurants, retail — where one person has to be the strategist, the engineer, and the one who pushes the deploy. Then I ran paid digital for Landry's: 60+ brands, 500+ locations, one of the largest hospitality companies in the country.

That work became a question I couldn't put down: why do the businesses we walk into every week know less about us than a website we visited once? Frequentor is my answer — and building it solo meant learning ESP32 firmware, mmWave radar, React Native internals, AWS IAM, LLM fine-tuning, and USPTO patent drafting in a single year. The other ten builds on this page are what that velocity looks like everywhere else — startups, client platforms, and the day-job improvisations in between.
